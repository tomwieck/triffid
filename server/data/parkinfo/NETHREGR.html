Netham Park and Pavilion

Large park and green space in East Bristol with leisure, sport and recreation facilities.

Admission and opening times

There is no charge for admission. The park is open at all times.

Pavilion opening times:

  • Saturday: 1pm to 4pm for pre-booked sports fixtures
  • Sunday: 10am to 1pm for pre-booked sports fixtures

Location

Netham Park, Avonvale Rd, Netham, Bristol, BS5 9RZ

Parking

There is a dedicated car park off Avonvale Road, Redfield. It is open at all times.

There is also a car park by the pavilion, which is only open during sports fixtures and events.

Disabled access

Park access is limited as there are no footpaths around the lower plateau. The Pavilion is fully accessible.
