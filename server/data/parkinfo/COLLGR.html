Peaceful city centre green space near to local attractions.

Admission and opening times

There is no charge for admission. College Green is open at all times.

Location

College Green, Bristol, BS1 5UY

Parking

There is no dedicated car park. Find out where to park in Bristol.

Disabled access

College Green has wheelchair accessible pathways.

There is an accessible toilet in the nearby Central Library.

Facilities and features
