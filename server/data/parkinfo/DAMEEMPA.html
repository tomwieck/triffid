Admission and opening times

There is no charge for admission. Dame Emily Park is open at all times.

Location

Dame Emily Park, Dean Lane, Bedminster, BS3 1BS

Parking

There is no dedicated car park. Find out where to park in Bristol.

Disabled access

Dame Emily Park is accessible, but there are some gentle slopes.

Facilities and features

  • dog-free playground for under 10s with a zip-wire for older children
  • wheels park for BMX, skateboards and scooters
  • basketball court
  • community garden (managed by Dame Emily Park Project)

Community support

The Dame Emily Park Project was started by local people to support the park. For more information or to join visit the Dame Emily Park Facebook page.
