St Agnes Park

Popular community park with playground.

Admission and opening times

There is no charge for admission. St Agnes Park is open at all times.

Location

St. Agnes Park, Thomas Street, Montpelier, Bristol, BS2 9QJ

Parking

There is no dedicated car park. Find out where to park in Bristol.

Disabled access

St Agnes Park is accessible for wheelchairs.

Facilities and features

  • dog-free playground
  • public toilets nearby at Cabot Circus, BS1 3BX

Community support

St Pauls Unlimited is a community group that supports the park. For more information or to join visit the St Pauls Unlimited website.
