Admission and opening times

There is no admission charge. Greville Smyth Park is open at all times.

Location

Greville Smyth Park, Ashton Road, Ashton, Bristol BS3 2EA

Parking

There is no dedicated car park. Find out where to park in Bristol.

Disabled access

The park is on different levels, so some paths are not suitable for wheelchairs.

Facilities and features

  • dog-free play area for under 12s
  • dog-free play area for teenagers
  • three football pitches
  • one ping pong table
  • tennis courts managed by Greville Smyth Tennis Club
  • five outdoor exercise stations
  • bowling green managed by Greville Smyth Community Bowls Club

Activities

There are outdoor fitness sessions run by Calon Personal Training

Community support

Friends of Greville Smyth (FROGS) is a community group that supports the park. For more information or to join visit the FROGS website.
