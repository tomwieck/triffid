Small Victorian park in a suburb of north Bristol.

Admission and opening times

There is no charge for admission. Cotham Gardens is open at all times.

Location

Redland Grove, Redland, Bristol BS6 6AG

Parking

There is no dedicated car park. Find out where to park in Bristol.

Disabled access

Cotham Gardens is accessible, but there are some gentle slopes.

Facilities and features

There is a large dog-free play area for under 12s.

Community support

The Redland & Cotham Amenities Society is a community group that supports the park. For more information or to join visit the Redland & Cotham Amenities Society website.
