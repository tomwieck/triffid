Classic Victorian neighbourhood park.

Admission and opening times

There is no charge for admission. St Andrews Park is open at all times.

Location

St Andrews Park, Effingham Road, St Andrews, Bristol BS6

Parking

There is no dedicated car park. Find out where to park in Bristol.

Disabled access

To find out about accessibility at St Andrews Park, you can look at an access guide on DisabledGo.

Facilities and features

  • dog-free children’s play area
  • outdoor bowling green managed by St Andrews and Kildare Bowling Club
  • paddling pool for children (unsupervised), open from the first May bank holiday to September
  • toilets

Toilet opening hours

Monday to Sunday: 8.15am to 6.15pm

Closed: 25 and 26 December and 1 January

Community support

The Friends of St Andrews Park is a community group that supports the park. For more information or to join visit the Friends of St Andrews Park website.

Events

The Friends of St Andrews Park holds events throughout the year. See what’s on at the Friends of St Andrews Park website.
