Large community park in St George with children’s playground and football pitches.

Admission and opening times

There is no charge for admission. Dundridge Park is open at all times.

Location

Dundridge Lane, Bristol, BS5 8SW

Parking

Dedicated car park is open 7 days a week 

Disabled access

The entrance gate is wide enough for most disability scooters.  There is a level path along one side of the park and to the children’s play area.

Facilities and features

  • two football pitches (bookable on Saturdays and Sundays)
  • artificial cricket pitch
  • outdoor seating
  • children’s play area
  • woodland areas
  • bridleway

Model aircraft, including drones

Under the park byelaws, you can fly model aircraft, including drones, in part of Dundridge Park (pdf, 140k) (opens new window) .

The model aircraft must weigh 7kg or less, without its fuel. The person flying the aircraft must be a member of the British Model Flying Association and have public liability insurance.
