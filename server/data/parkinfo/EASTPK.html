Admission and opening times

There is no admission charge. Eastville Park is open at all times.

Location

Eastville Park, Fishponds Road, Eastville, Bristol, BS5

Parking

You can park at Eastville Park car park, at Park Avenue, BS5 6QL.

Check signs at the car parks for exact closing time as this changes throughout the year.

Open hours

Monday to Friday: 7.30am to dusk
Saturday to Sunday: 8:30am to dusk

Car park closed on 25 December.

Disabled access

To find out about accessibility at Eastville Park, you can look at an access guide on DisabledGo.

Facilities and features

  • fishing lake
  • dog-free playground for under 15s with basketball area
  • play area for under 12s
  • three football pitches
  • tennis court
  • one mile running circuit
  • one bowling green
